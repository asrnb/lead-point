# General FAQs

**Do you treat children?**
Yes, ages 3 and up. See the Services document for details on our pediatric
scope and when we refer out.

**Can I get an appointment the same week I call?**
Usually yes for new patients, and often same-day for genuine dental
emergencies — see the Emergency Care document.

**Do you offer sedation?**
We offer nitrous oxide ("laughing gas") for anxious patients. We do not
offer IV sedation or general anesthesia in-office; those cases are referred
to an oral surgeon's office that has the appropriate facilities.

**What if I'm nervous about the dentist?**
Let us know when you book — we can schedule extra time, offer nitrous oxide,
and Dr. Nair in particular has a lot of experience with anxious patients.

**Do you accept walk-ins?**
Not for routine care — we're appointment-only to keep wait times low.
Genuine emergencies during office hours are the exception; call ahead if you
can, even a few minutes' notice helps us prepare.

**Can I switch dentists within the practice?**
Yes, just let the front desk know your preference for future visits.

**Is there parking?**
Yes, free parking in the lot behind the building — see the Hours & Location
document.

**Do you do virtual/telehealth consultations?**
Not currently — dentistry generally requires an in-person exam, so we don't
offer virtual visits at this time.
