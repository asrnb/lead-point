# Insurance

Riverside Dental Clinic is in-network with the following PPO plans:

- Delta Dental PPO
- Cigna Dental PPO
- MetLife PPO
- Guardian PPO

We are **out-of-network** with all HMO/DMO plans and with Medicaid. If you
have an HMO or DMO plan, we can still see you as a self-pay (uninsured)
patient, but we cannot bill your insurance directly.

## How it works
- Bring your insurance card (physical or a photo) to your first visit.
- We verify your benefits before your appointment and let you know your
  estimated out-of-pocket cost in advance.
- We file claims on your behalf for in-network PPO plans; you're responsible
  for whatever your plan doesn't cover (deductible, coinsurance, or anything
  above your annual maximum).
- If you're not sure whether your specific plan is in-network, the front desk
  can check for you before you book — we'd rather confirm than have you
  surprised by a bill.
