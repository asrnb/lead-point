# New Patients

## What to bring
- Photo ID
- Insurance card (if applicable)
- List of current medications
- Any recent X-rays from a prior dentist (we can also request these directly
  from your previous office with your permission)

## Your first visit
Plan for about 60–75 minutes. It includes a full exam, necessary X-rays, a
cleaning (if time allows and there's no active decay needing treatment
first), and a conversation about any concerns or goals you have. You'll leave
with a written treatment plan and pricing for anything beyond the exam.

## New patient paperwork
We'll email you a link to fill out your health history and consent forms
online before your visit — this saves about 15 minutes of paperwork in the
waiting room. If you'd rather fill it out in person, that's fine too, just
plan to arrive 15 minutes early.

## Booking your first appointment
You can book directly through the chat widget on this site, or call the
office. First-time patients are usually able to get an appointment within a
week.
