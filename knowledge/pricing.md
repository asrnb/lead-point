# Pricing

Prices below are self-pay (uninsured) estimates for the most common services.
Actual cost depends on your specific case and is confirmed at your exam —
these are starting prices, not quotes.

| Service | Starting price |
|---|---|
| New patient exam + X-rays | $89 |
| Routine cleaning (returning patient) | $120 |
| Composite filling (1 surface) | $175 |
| Root canal (front tooth) | $650 |
| Root canal (molar) | $950 |
| Porcelain crown | $1,100 |
| Tooth extraction (simple) | $150 |
| Teeth whitening (in-office) | $350 |
| Invisalign (full treatment) | starting at $3,500 |
| Single dental implant (post + crown) | starting at $3,200 |

## Payment options
- We accept cash, all major credit cards, and CareCredit financing (subject to
  CareCredit's own approval and terms).
- A 5% discount is available for patients who pay in full, same-day, without
  insurance.
- We do not currently offer an in-house payment plan beyond CareCredit.

## What affects the final price
- X-rays, the number of surfaces filled, whether a case needs a specialist
  referral, and lab fees for crowns/dentures all affect the final price.
- We always provide a written treatment plan with exact pricing before any
  work begins — nothing is billed without your sign-off first.
