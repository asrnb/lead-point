# Services

Riverside Dental Clinic offers general, cosmetic, and restorative dentistry for
patients of all ages.

## General Dentistry
- **Routine cleanings & exams**: recommended every 6 months, includes a full
  exam, plaque/tartar removal, and polish.
- **Fillings**: tooth-colored composite fillings for cavities, completed in a
  single visit.
- **Root canals**: performed in-house for adults; complex cases are referred to
  a trusted endodontist partner.
- **Extractions**: routine extractions in-office; wisdom teeth extractions are
  referred to an oral surgeon partner.

## Cosmetic Dentistry
- **Teeth whitening**: in-office whitening (about 1 hour) or take-home trays.
- **Veneers**: porcelain veneers for chipped, discolored, or gapped teeth.
- **Invisalign**: clear aligner treatment for mild-to-moderate misalignment.

## Restorative Dentistry
- **Crowns & bridges**: same-day crowns available using in-office milling for
  most cases.
- **Dental implants**: single-tooth implants placed and restored in-house;
  full-arch implant cases are referred to a specialist.
- **Dentures**: full and partial dentures, including same-day repairs.

## Pediatric Dentistry
- Cleanings and exams for children ages 3+. We do not currently have a
  pediatric specialist on staff — patients under 3 or with complex pediatric
  needs are referred to a pediatric dental partner.

For anything not listed here (for example, sleep apnea appliances or complex
oral surgery), the front desk can advise whether we treat it in-house or refer
out.
